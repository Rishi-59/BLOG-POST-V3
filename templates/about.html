About Me

Hi, I'm Kunal More, a Computer Science graduate and aspiring AI Engineer from Pune, India.

I created this blog as my first full-stack Flask project—not just to learn web development, but to document my journey as I continue growing as a software developer. Every feature on this website has helped me learn something new, from routing and templates to user authentication, databases, and deployment.

I'm passionate about building intelligent software that solves real-world problems. My primary interests include Python, Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, backend development, and automation. I enjoy exploring new technologies, solving challenging programming problems, and continuously improving my skills through hands-on projects.

Why This Blog?

This blog is my digital notebook—a place where I document what I learn, share my projects, and reflect on my journey as a developer. Writing about what I build helps reinforce my understanding while hopefully helping others who are learning as well.

Here you'll find posts about:

  • Python programming
  • Flask and backend development
  • Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning
  • Data Structures & Algorithms
  • Software development tips
  • Personal projects and experiments

Beyond Coding

When I'm not coding, you'll usually find me solving DSA problems, learning new technologies, experimenting with AI projects, or planning the next idea I want to build.

Looking Ahead

My goal is to become a skilled AI Engineer by combining strong software engineering fundamentals with practical AI applications. Every project I build teaches me something new, and this blog is where I share that journey.

Thank you for stopping by. I hope you find something useful, interesting, or inspiring here.
